THE WORLD UNDERWATER

Mother Earth gave human everything but human abused every boon. Eventually all the glaciers melted and whole earth surface except mountain peaks submerged. The feedback effect (chain evaporation and precipitation vicious cycle increasing greenhouse effect) made it impossible for humanity to live on surface. So the world went underwater rebuilding their lives, facing new challenges, learning new things, fighting over resources as again the survival instinct took hold of humanity.
Let's dive deeper into their lives and behold how their instincts have answered to the new challenges.
